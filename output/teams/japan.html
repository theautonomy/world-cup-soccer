🗺️ 地理与文化 🗺️ Geography & Culture

日本是位于东亚的岛国，由本州、北海道、四国、九州四大岛及数千个小岛组成，国土面积约37.8万平方公里，人口约1.25亿。日本以其独特的传统文化（茶道、武道、能剧）与现代科技的高度融合享誉全球，东京是全球最繁华的都市之一，富士山则是这片土地上的精神象征。

Japan is an island nation in East Asia consisting of four main islands — Honshu, Hokkaido, Shikoku, and Kyushu — along with thousands of smaller islands, covering approximately 378,000 km² with a population of around 125 million. Renowned for its seamless blend of ancient tradition (tea ceremony, martial arts, Noh theatre) and cutting-edge modernity, Japan is home to Tokyo, one of the world's most dynamic cities, and the iconic Mount Fuji.

⚽ 足球文化 ⚽ Football Culture

日本足球在过去30年经历了翻天覆地的变革。1993年J联赛的创立标志着职业化的起点，随后快速提升的青训体系和大批旅欧球员推动日本队晋升亚洲顶级。2022年卡塔尔世界杯，"蓝色武士"在同一组击败卫冕世界杯冠军德国（2-1）和欧洲杯冠军西班牙（2-1），震惊世界。如今，日本队阵中活跃着一批效力欧洲顶级联赛的球员，正以前所未有的实力冲击2026年的更高突破。

Japanese football has undergone a remarkable transformation over the past 30 years. The launch of the J.League in 1993 sparked professional football's rise, and a strong youth development system combined with a generation of Europe-based players has catapulted Japan to the top of Asian football. At the 2022 World Cup, the "Samurai Blue" stunned the world by beating reigning champions Germany (2-1) and European champions Spain (2-1) in the same group. Today, with more players than ever in Europe's top leagues, Japan enters 2026 chasing their first quarterfinal.
