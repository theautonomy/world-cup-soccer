🗺️ 地理与文化 🗺️ Geography & Culture

巴西是南美洲最大的国家，国土面积居世界第五，拥有亚马逊雨林与广袤的热带草原。作为葡萄牙语国家中人口最多的国家，巴西文化融合了欧洲、非洲与原住民文明的精髓，孕育出桑巴、狂欢节等举世闻名的文化符号。

Brazil is the largest country in South America and the fifth largest in the world, home to the Amazon rainforest and vast cerrado savannahs. The most populous Portuguese-speaking nation, Brazil's vibrant culture blends European, African, and indigenous heritage, giving rise to iconic traditions like samba and Carnival.

⚽ 足球文化 ⚽ Football Culture

足球在巴西不仅仅是运动，更是一种民族信仰。巴西街头的"弄堂球"（futsal）文化培育出无数天才球员，"桑巴足球"以其独特的个人技巧、创意与欢乐著称于世。巴西拥有全球最多的世界杯冠军，是唯一参加过每一届世界杯的国家。

Football in Brazil is more than a sport — it's a national religion. The street football (futsal) culture has produced countless gifted players, and "samba football" is celebrated worldwide for its unique flair, creativity, and joy. Brazil holds the most World Cup titles of any nation and is the only country to have participated in every single World Cup.

巴西 Brazil vs 阿根廷 Argentina
南美经典宿敌，被誉为"美洲超级德比"。两国之间的对决不仅是足球竞争，更是文化与民族自尊的碰撞。双方在南美解放者杯、世界杯和美洲杯的历次交锋，每一场都牵动着数以亿计球迷的心跳。
South America's greatest derby, dubbed the "Superclásico de las Américas." Their clashes transcend football — they are battles of culture and national pride. Every meeting at Copa América, World Cup qualifiers, or the World Cup itself grips billions of fans worldwide.
巴西 Brazil vs 德国 Germany
2014年世界杯半决赛，德国在巴西主场以7:1大胜，这场被称为"米内罗惨案"的比赛令巴西全国陷入震惊与悲痛，成为世界杯历史上最难以置信的比赛之一。两队在世界杯上曾多次狭路相逢，各有胜负。
The 2014 semi-final "Mineirazo" — Germany's 7-1 demolition of Brazil on home soil — left the nation in shock and grief, becoming one of the most shocking results in World Cup history. The two sides have met multiple times at the tournament, with memorable contests on both sides.
巴西 Brazil vs 荷兰 Netherlands
两支同样崇尚进攻美学的球队之间的对决，往往贡献世界杯最精彩的比赛。1974年西德世界杯克鲁伊夫领衔的荷兰淘汰巴西，2010年南非世界杯荷兰再度在八强将其淘汰，每次碰面都充满戏剧性。
Two sides who share a love of attacking football but have clashed memorably at the World Cup. Cruyff's Netherlands eliminated Brazil in 1974, and the Dutch did it again in 2010. Their meetings are always filled with quality, drama, and goals.

关于巴西的趣味数据 Fascinating facts about Brazil

🌍
巴西是世界上唯一参加过每一届FIFA世界杯（共22届，1930–2026）的国家，是当之无愧的"世界杯常青树"。
Brazil is the only country to have qualified for every single FIFA World Cup — all 22 editions from 1930 to 2026. No other nation comes close to this record.
🏆
巴西以5次世界杯冠军雄居榜首，比排名第二的德国（4次）还多一次。他们在1958、1962、1970年完成"三连霸"时代，至今无人复制。
Brazil's five World Cup titles are the most of any nation — one more than second-placed Germany (4). Their near-dynasty spanning 1958, 1962, and 1970 has never been replicated by any other nation.
😱
2014年主场世界杯半决赛，巴西以1:7惨败德国，其中45分钟内连丢5球，这被称为"米内罗惨案"（Mineirazo），是世界杯史上最震惊的比赛之一。
In the 2014 semi-final on home soil, Brazil were thrashed 7-1 by Germany, conceding 5 goals in just 18 minutes. The "Mineirazo" remains one of the most shocking results in World Cup history.
👶
贝利是世界杯历史上年龄最小的冠军之一。他在1958年世界杯首球时年仅17岁零249天，决赛帽子戏法令全世界惊叹，至今仍是最年轻的世界杯决赛进球者。
Pelé is one of the youngest World Cup winners ever. Just 17 years and 249 days old at his first World Cup in 1958, his hat-trick in the final astonished the world. He remains the youngest player to score in a World Cup final.
⚽
巴西在世界杯历史上共打进237球，是所有参赛国中进球最多的球队，并且拥有76场胜利的世界杯历史最多胜场纪录。
Brazil have scored 237 goals at World Cups — the most of any nation — and hold the all-time record for World Cup wins with 76 victories. Their goal difference of +129 is also the best in history.
