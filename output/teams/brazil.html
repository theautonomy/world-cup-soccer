🗺️ 地理与文化 🗺️ Geography & Culture

巴西联邦共和国是南美洲最大的国家，国土面积居世界第五，人口约2.17亿。亚马逊雨林覆盖其北部广大地区，孕育了地球上最丰富的生物多样性。巴西以狂欢节、桑巴舞、热带海滩和多元文化闻名于世，是一个充满活力与激情的南美大国。

Brazil, officially the Federative Republic of Brazil, is the largest country in South America and the fifth largest in the world by area, with a population of approximately 217 million. The Amazon rainforest dominates its northern regions, harboring the planet's most extraordinary biodiversity. Known for its carnival, samba, stunning coastlines, and vibrant multicultural identity, Brazil is one of the world's most colorful and energetic nations.

⚽ 足球文化 ⚽ Football Culture

足球在巴西不仅是运动，更是民族信仰。巴西是世界上唯一一支参加了每一届世界杯的球队，共夺得五次冠军。"美丽足球"（Jogo Bonito）代表着巴西独特的踢球哲学——技术华丽、节奏流畅、充满创造力。里约热内卢的马拉卡纳球场是世界足球的精神圣地，1950年世界杯的"马拉卡纳惨案"至今仍是全国最深的集体伤痛。

Football in Brazil is more than a sport — it is a national religion. Brazil is the only team to have participated in every single FIFA World Cup, claiming the title five times. "Jogo Bonito" (The Beautiful Game) captures Brazil's footballing philosophy: dazzling skill, fluid rhythm, and boundless creativity. The Maracanã in Rio de Janeiro is football's spiritual home, and the 1950 "Maracanazo" defeat to Uruguay remains the nation's deepest footballing wound.

足球场上最激烈的对决 The most intense battles on the pitch

巴西 Brazil vs 阿根廷 🇦🇷 Argentina 🇦🇷
南美超级德比——"美洲超级德比"（Superclássico das Américas）是世界足球史上最激烈的双边对抗之一。两国之间的竞争超越了足球本身，涵盖历史、文化与民族自尊。两队在世界杯赛场上多次相遇，交锋从来火花四溅。近年来，随着梅西与维尼修斯等超级球星的对决，这一宿敌格局更加精彩。
The "Superclássico das Américas" is one of football's fiercest bilateral rivalries, transcending sport into history, culture, and national pride. Brazil and Argentina have met repeatedly on the World Cup stage, with every clash carrying immense weight. With modern icons like Messi and Vinicius Jr. squaring off, this rivalry has never burned hotter.
巴西 Brazil vs 乌拉圭 🇺🇾 Uruguay 🇺🇾
1950年世界杯决赛，20万球迷聚集马拉卡纳，坐等巴西捧杯——但乌拉圭以2-0上演世纪大逆转，留下了"马拉卡纳惨案"（Maracanazo）这一永恒的历史伤痛。这场失利对巴西人的心理冲击之深，相当于一次国家级的集体创伤。
In the 1950 World Cup final round, 200,000 fans packed the Maracanã expecting Brazil to be crowned — but Uruguay stunned the hosts 2-0 in what became the "Maracanazo." This defeat left a psychological scar so deep it is considered a national trauma, making every Brazil-Uruguay encounter charged with historical weight.
巴西 Brazil vs 德国 🇩🇪 Germany 🇩🇪
2014年世界杯半决赛，巴西在主场以1-7惨败于德国，创造了"米内罗惨案"（Mineirazo）——这不仅是巴西足球历史上最惨痛的失利，也是世界杯历史上最震撼的结果之一。赛场上数万观众留下的泪水和沉默，定格为足球史上最难忘的画面之一。
The 2014 World Cup semifinal at the Estádio Mineirão produced the "Mineirazo" — Brazil's catastrophic 1-7 collapse on home soil against Germany. Tens of thousands of Brazilians wept openly in the stands. This historic humiliation redefined the Brazil-Germany rivalry and remains one of the most shocking results in World Cup history.

🌍
巴西是世界上唯一一支参加了每一届FIFA世界杯的球队，从1930年首届世界杯至今，从未缺席，出场22次。
Brazil is the only nation to have participated in every single FIFA World Cup — appearing in all 22 tournaments since 1930 without ever failing to qualify.
🏆
巴西以5次世界杯冠军稳居历史第一，并在欧洲（1958）、南美洲（1950/1962）、北美洲（1970/1994）和亚洲（2002）四大洲夺冠，是唯一在四个大洲称王的球队。
Brazil's 5 World Cup titles are the most in history. They are also the only team to have won the tournament on four different continents: Europe (1958), South America (1950/1962), North America (1970/1994), and Asia (2002).
⚽
1970年巴西队至今被许多球迷和专家公认为世界杯历史上最伟大的球队，那支球队拥有贝利、加林查、里维利诺、托斯唐等超级巨星，全程6战全胜，共打入19球。
The 1970 Brazil team is widely regarded as the greatest World Cup squad ever assembled. Featuring Pelé, Jairzinho, Rivelino, and Tostão, they won all 6 matches and scored 19 goals on their way to lifting the Jules Rimet Trophy permanently.
😢
2014年世界杯半决赛，巴西在本土以1-7惨败于德国，这场"米内罗惨案"创下了多项耻辱纪录，包括巴西队史世界杯最大比分失利，半场便已0-5落后。
In the 2014 World Cup semifinal on home soil, Brazil were demolished 1-7 by Germany — the "Mineirazo." Germany scored five goals in 18 minutes in the first half. It remains Brazil's worst ever World Cup defeat and one of the most shocking results in football history.
👟
马拉卡纳球场于1950年世界杯为见证巴西夺冠而建，当年决赛观众人数估计超过20万，至今仍是世界杯史上最高的单场观众纪录。
The Maracanã was built for the 1950 World Cup, where an estimated 200,000+ fans witnessed the "Maracanazo" — still the largest crowd in World Cup history. The stadium became a symbol of both Brazilian football's glory and its greatest heartbreak.
