🗺️ 地理与文化 🗺️ Geography & Culture

西班牙王国位于欧洲西南部，占据伊比利亚半岛大部分地区，国土面积约50.6万平方公里，人口约4740万。西班牙以其悠久的历史、多元的地域文化、灿烂的艺术遗产和热情奔放的生活方式享誉全球。弗拉明戈舞蹈、斗牛传统、建筑巨匠高迪的奇幻杰作，共同构成了这片土地独特的文明景观。

The Kingdom of Spain occupies the southwestern tip of Europe on the Iberian Peninsula, covering approximately 506,000 km² with a population of around 47.4 million. Spain is celebrated for its rich history, diverse regional cultures, vibrant arts heritage, and passionate way of life. From flamenco dancing to Gaudí's architectural masterpieces, Spain's civilization is as colorful as its football jersey.

⚽ 足球文化 ⚽ Football Culture

西班牙是欧洲足球最深厚底蕴的国度之一，拥有皇家马德里和FC巴塞罗那两支全球最具影响力的俱乐部。2008年至2012年，西班牙国家队以"蒂基-塔卡"传控战术横扫世界，完成欧洲杯-世界杯-欧洲杯的史诗三连冠。2024年，西班牙再夺欧洲杯，成为欧洲杯历史上夺冠次数最多的球队（4次），携带拉明·亚马尔等新生代天才昂首冲击2026世界杯。

Spain is home to two of the world's most powerful clubs — Real Madrid and FC Barcelona — and its football culture runs deep. Between 2008-2012, La Roja dominated world football with "tiki-taka," completing a historic Euro-World Cup-Euro treble. In 2024, Spain claimed a record fourth European Championship, announcing the next generation of stars like Lamine Yamal to the world as they head into 2026 as genuine title contenders.

西班牙 Spain vs 葡萄牙 🇵🇹 Portugal 🇵🇹
"伊比利亚德比"是欧洲最悠久的双边国家队对抗之一，两支同处伊比利亚半岛的邻居在世界杯和欧洲杯赛场多次相遇。两国各拥有顶级球星云集的队伍，每次交锋均是欧洲足球的一场盛宴，2018年世界杯小组赛3-3平局更被视为近年来最精彩的比赛之一。
The "Iberian Derby" is one of Europe's oldest national team rivalries. These Iberian Peninsula neighbors have clashed at multiple World Cups and European Championships. Their 2018 World Cup group stage 3-3 draw — featuring a Cristiano Ronaldo hat-trick — is considered one of the greatest matches in recent World Cup history.
西班牙 Spain vs 德国 🇩🇪 Germany 🇩🇪
西班牙与德国是欧洲顶级强队之间的传统对抗，两队在多届大赛中正面交锋。2008年欧洲杯决赛西班牙1-0击败德国，开启黄金时代；2010年世界杯半决赛再度1-0淘汰德国，奠定最终夺冠。两队之间的较量向来是欧洲足球的至高舞台之一。
Spain vs Germany is one of European football's great heavyweight clashes. Spain beat Germany 1-0 in the Euro 2008 final, launching their golden era, then knocked Germany out 1-0 in the 2010 World Cup semi-final on the way to their historic title. Their battles consistently represent the best of European football.
西班牙 Spain vs 意大利 🇮🇹 Italy 🇮🇹
南欧两强之间的对抗横跨数十年。西班牙与意大利在世界杯历史上多次短兵相接，包括1994年八强、2012年欧洲杯决赛（4-0横扫）等经典对决。两队的踢法哲学截然不同——西班牙崇尚传控，意大利信奉防守——使每次对阵充满战术博弈的吸引力。
Spain and Italy have a storied rivalry spanning decades. Clashes like the 1994 World Cup quarterfinal, and the brutal 4-0 Euro 2012 final thrashing of Italy, are etched into history. Their contrasting philosophies — Spain's tiki-taka vs Italy's catenaccio — make every meeting a compelling tactical duel.

关于西班牙的趣味数据 Fascinating facts about Spain

🥇
2008年至2012年，西班牙完成欧洲杯—世界杯—欧洲杯的史诗三连冠，成为历史上第一支完成这一壮举的球队。4年内共获得三个大赛冠军，打造了国际足球史上最强盛的王朝之一。
Between 2008 and 2012, Spain achieved the unprecedented feat of winning Euro 2008 → WC 2010 → Euro 2012 — the first team in history to win three consecutive major international titles. Their dynasty defined an era of football.
🏆
西班牙是欧洲杯历史上夺冠次数最多的球队，共夺得4次欧洲杯冠军（1964、2008、2012、2024），超越德国（3次），成为欧洲大陆的最强王者。
Spain have won a record four UEFA European Championships (1964, 2008, 2012, 2024), more than any other nation. Their 2024 Euro victory, featuring a new generation of stars, confirmed Spain's continued dominance at the top level of European football.
🌟
2024年欧洲杯，拉明·亚马尔在参加决赛时年仅17岁，成为欧洲杯历史上年龄最小的进球者和出场球员。他的横空出世宣告了西班牙足球新时代的到来，并以当届赛事最佳年轻球员的身份荣耀加冕。
At Euro 2024, Lamine Yamal became the youngest player and scorer in European Championship history at just 17. His emergence announced a thrilling new era for Spanish football — he was named Best Young Player of the tournament and finished runner-up for the Ballon d'Or shortly after.
🤙
西班牙国歌《皇家进行曲》是全球少数几首没有歌词的国歌之一。正因如此，西班牙球员在比赛前集体沉默或拍手，与其他国家球员高声合唱的场景形成鲜明对比，成为赛场上的一道独特风景。
Spain's national anthem, the "Marcha Real," is one of the few anthems in the world with no official lyrics. As a result, Spanish players stand in silence or clap during the pre-match anthem — a striking contrast to opponents singing in full voice, and a quirky piece of Spanish footballing identity.
🔴
塞尔希奥·拉莫斯以180次出场成为欧洲足球历史上出场次数最多的球员（至其退役时），同时也是世界杯历史上黄牌最多的球员之一。他标志性的后卫统治力和大赛关键时刻的进球能力，是西班牙黄金时代的守卫基石。
Sergio Ramos retired with 180 international caps — a European record — and was also the most yellow-carded player in World Cup history. His commanding defensive presence, combined with crucial goals in big matches, made him the defensive cornerstone of Spain's golden generation.
